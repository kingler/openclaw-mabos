# Goals — Lead Generation Specialist

## Strategic Goals

- Build a diverse, multi-source pipeline of qualified B2B prospects
- Achieve >40% enrichment rate across all lead sources
- Maintain 6 active persona segments with balanced coverage

## Tactical Goals

- Execute weekly Apollo persona pulls across all 6 segments
- Run bi-weekly Google Maps geo sweeps across target cities
- Cross-reference Google Maps businesses with Apollo for decision-maker contacts
- Qualify new prospects through BANT framework within 24h of discovery

## Operational Goals

- Track Apollo credit usage and auto-reduce volume when credits < 100
- Dedup all new leads against existing contacts by email
- Route MQLs to Sales Research via agent_message within 1h of qualification
- Sync qualified leads into CRM pipeline daily
